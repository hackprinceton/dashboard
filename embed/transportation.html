Parking

Please park in Lot 21 (shown on the "Maps" tab above) and follow the signs to get taken to the main venue. You do not need a parking or overnight permit.
For a more detailed parking map, go to http://www.princeton.edu/parking/Parking_Lots.pdf and you will find Lot 21 in the lower right hand corner of the main map.

From Newark (EWR)

Once you leave the secure area, follow signs for the AirTrain, the inter-terminal monorail. Board the AirTrain for the RailLink station. Upon arrival, head up the stairs to the left and cross the bridge to the train station. From the blue-and-orange machines, purchase a ticket on the NJTransit Northeast Corridor (NOT Northeast Regional) train to Princeton Junction (NOT Princeton Station). Then head to Track 5 to board the train towards Trenton. From Princeton Junction, the TigerPAWW shuttle bus (typically either a white minibus or an orange bus) provides free service to the TigerPAWW stop shown on our map. Exact train times can be looked up on the NJTransit site or on Google Maps.

From Philly Airport (PHL)

Take the train from the airport to 30th Street Station (the main station in Philly). Transfer here to the eastbound SEPTA train towards Trenton. At Trenton, transfer to the northbound NJTransit Northeast Corridor (NOT Northeast Regional) train towards Newark/New York City, disembarking at the Princeton Junction stop. From Princeton Junction, the TigerPAWW shuttle bus (typically either a white minibus or an orange bus) provides free service to the TigerPAWW stop shown on our map. Exact train times can be looked up on the NJTransit site or on Google Maps.
NOTE: The connector train ("Dinky") which normally runs between Princeton Junction and Princeton Station is undergoing construction this week. The free TigerPAWW shuttle bus is temporarily operating in its place.
The University website, and Google maps have additional details on all modes of transportation to campus: http://www.princeton.edu/main/visiting/travel/

From Penn Station (NYC)

Take the NJTransit Northeast Corridor (NOT Northeast Regional) train to Princeton Junction (NOT Princeton Station), boarding in the Trenton-bound direction. From Princeton Junction, the TigerPAWW shuttle bus (typically either a white minibus or an orange bus) provides free service to the TigerPAWW stop shown on our map. Exact train times can be looked up on the NJTransit site or on Google Maps.
