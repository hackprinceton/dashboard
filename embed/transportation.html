Parking

Please park in Lot 21 (shown on the "Maps" tab above) and follow the signs to get taken to the main venue. You do not need a parking or overnight permit.
For a more detailed parking map, go to http://www.princeton.edu/parking/Parking_Lots.pdf and you will find Lot 21 in the lower right hand corner of the main map.

NOTE: The connector train between Princeton Junction and Princeton University is undergoing maintenance. There will be NJTransit busses that will shuttle you between Princeton Junction Station and Princeton University.

From Penn Station (NYC)

Take one of the Northeast Corridor trains run by NJTransit to Princeton Junction. From Princeton Junction, you have the choice of a bus called TigerPAWW or another short train called that will take you to campus NJTransit shuttle busses. The on-campus station is listed as "Princeton University" (no Junction) on NJTransit's website. Exact train times can also be looked up on their site or Google Maps.

From Newark (EWR)

Take the Airport AirTrain to Newark Airport Rail Station, then one of the Northeast Corridor trains down to Princeton Junction station (and it's the same from there on).

From Philly Airport (PHL)

Take a train from the airport to 30th Street Station (the main station in Philly), then a train from 30th Street to Trenton, where you transfer to a NJTransit train which will take you to Princeton Junction (and it's the same from there on).
The University website, and Google maps have additional details on all modes of transportation to campus: http://www.princeton.edu/main/visiting/travel/
