Arriving

Take the Newark Airport AirTrain towards Newark Airport Rail Station.
Once there, purchase tickets from the NJ Transit kiosk departing from EWR to Princeton (Not Princeton Junction).
Board the NJ Transit train headed in the direction of Trenton.
Exit the train at Princeton Junction. Transfer to the 2-car train headed towards Princeton, aka "Dinky."

Departing

Arriving

Take the SEPTA R1 train from the airport towards the Philadephia 30th Street Station.
Transfer to the SEPTA R7 train to Trenton Station.
Transfer to the NJ Transit Northeast Corridor line northbound towards Princeton Junction.
Exit at Princeton Junction. Transfer to the NJ TRANSIT headed towards Princeton (a.k.a. Dinky).
